# Contributing to CanopyOps

CanopyOps welcomes upstream reports and improvements that make the canonical Collaborative Dynamics release safer, clearer, more useful, or more portable.

## Start with an issue

Open the matching [CanopyOps issue](https://github.com/Stunspot/CanopyOps/issues/new/choose) before substantial work:

- bug report;
- source-currency concern;
- capability request.

This lets maintainers confirm scope, evidence needs, release fit, and whether a change belongs in authored Augment material or MIT-licensed software.

## Good contributions

- reproducible fixes to scripts or schemas;
- corrected calculations and edge cases;
- current primary-source corrections with jurisdiction and date;
- clearer customer documentation;
- accessibility improvements;
- safer authority, emergency, and refusal routing;
- additional synthetic examples or discriminating evaluations;
- host adapters that preserve the CanopyOps trust boundary.

## Evidence expectations

Explain the problem, affected file, proposed behavior, and how the change was checked. Use synthetic or sanitized cases. A horticultural, legal, regulatory, label, safety, or laboratory correction should identify the current primary source and its scope.

Do not submit confidential facility material, personal data, credentials, proprietary recipes, copyrighted third-party text, or output you do not have the right to contribute.

## License and identity

By submitting a contribution, you represent that you have the right to submit it and agree that Collaborative Dynamics may include it in CanopyOps under the license applicable to that part of the package:

- MIT for Python scripts and machine-readable schemas;
- `CC-BY-ND-4.0` for authored Augment material and documentation.

The no-derivatives term governs public distribution of modified CanopyOps outside the canonical project. It does not prevent proposing changes upstream for inclusion in a new official release.

CanopyOps, Collaborative Dynamics, Ella Greenfield, stunspot, and supplied product marks remain governed by [TRADEMARKS.md](TRADEMARKS.md).

## Review and acceptance

Maintainers may request changes, rewrite a contribution for package consistency, decline it, or defer it. Submission does not guarantee inclusion, attribution beyond the project records, support, compensation, or a release date.
