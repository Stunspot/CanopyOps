# Support

CanopyOps is a free Collaborative Dynamics Augment. Support is best-effort and routed through the repository that distributes this release.

## Use the right issue type

- **Bug report:** installation, packaging, broken links, incorrect deterministic output, missing files, or behavior that contradicts the documented workflow.
- **Source-currency concern:** a legal, regulatory, label, safety, laboratory, horticultural, or equipment claim may be stale, mis-scoped, or weakly sourced.
- **Capability request:** a proposed workflow, artifact, calculation, adapter, or quality-of-life improvement.

Use [CanopyOps Issues](https://github.com/Stunspot/CanopyOps/issues/new/choose) and choose the matching template.

## Include useful evidence

For software or packaging defects, include:

- CanopyOps version and installation method;
- host and operating system;
- exact command or request used;
- expected and actual result;
- a minimal fictional or sanitized reproduction;
- relevant error text.

For source-currency concerns, include the affected file and claim, jurisdiction or scope, date checked, and a current primary source when available.

## Keep sensitive information out of public issues

Do not post facility addresses, access details, employee or customer data, license identifiers, security controls, proprietary recipes, private logs, API keys, credentials, precise inventory, or unredacted compliance records.

Reduce the problem to fictional or sanitized facts. If that is impossible, ask Collaborative Dynamics for a private reporting route through https://collaborative-dynamics.com before transmitting the material.

## Not an emergency or professional-help channel

Repository support is not appropriate for active CO2 or life-safety alarms, worker exposure, fire, electrical or structural hazards, crop emergencies requiring immediate qualified action, legal deadlines, medical questions, pesticide authorization, or batch-release decisions.

Use the facility emergency procedure and the appropriate qualified responder, regulator, attorney, laboratory, safety professional, equipment specialist, or accountable operator.

## Response expectations

An issue may be acknowledged, reproduced, requested for clarification, deferred, declined, or incorporated into a future canonical release. Opening an issue does not create a service-level agreement, consulting relationship, operational approval, or promise of a fix.
