# CanopyOps Glossary

- **Active target:** facility-applicable approved target for the stated room, stage, method, and time—not a generic recommended range.
- **Authority gate:** a point where the agent may prepare evidence but a named accountable human must decide.
- **Containment:** reversible action that limits harm across several live explanations without claiming cause.
- **Correction:** action directed at a sufficiently supported causal mechanism.
- **DLI:** daily light integral, cumulative PAR photons delivered per area per day.
- **Dryback:** decline in a defined substrate water measurement over a defined interval; meaningful only with method and baseline.
- **G×E:** genotype-by-environment interaction; performance belongs to both genetics and operating conditions.
- **Interpretation envelope:** the decisions the available evidence can responsibly support.
- **Leaf VPD:** difference between saturation vapor pressure at leaf temperature and ambient vapor pressure derived from air temperature/RH.
- **Live differential:** materially different explanations still capable of changing inquiry or treatment.
- **Operational foreground:** current condition, risk/hold, authority, next action, owner, and advance/reopen condition.
- **PPFD:** instantaneous photosynthetic photon flux density.
- **Provisional:** useful under explicit assumptions or missing evidence, but not ready for consequential reliance.
- **Runoff:** collected drainage from a defined irrigation event and sampling method; not automatically root-zone state.
- **Verified for stated use:** evidence supports the named use under recorded scope; not universal approval.
- **Water activity (aw):** method-defined measure of water’s energy/availability in a product; distinct from total moisture content.
