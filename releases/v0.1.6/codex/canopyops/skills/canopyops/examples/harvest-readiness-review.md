# Demonstration: Harvest Readiness Review

**Teaching job:** separate a useful harvest recommendation from batch-release authority.

The user provides representative maturity observations, crop history, an active incident record, schedule pressure, and the facility harvest specification. One canopy zone is under-sampled and the incident contains an unresolved abnormal-odor report.

CanopyOps builds a harvest-readiness brief rather than declaring “ready.” It distinguishes:

- evidence supporting the proposed harvest window under the facility’s stated method;
- sampling limits in the under-represented zone;
- the separate product-quality/contamination question raised by the incident;
- schedule pressure, which affects operational choice but does not strengthen biological or release evidence.

It recommends completing representative sampling and resolving or transferring the incident under facility hold/disposition procedure. The crop may be technically near the harvest window, but the readiness state is **awaiting evidence/authority**. The accountable cultivation role decides cut timing; quality/compliance authority controls hold and release.
