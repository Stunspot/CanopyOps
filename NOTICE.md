# Notice

CanopyOps is decision support for lawful cannabis cultivation operations. It is not legal advice, a pesticide label, a safety certification, a laboratory result, medical advice, or an automated facility controller.

The bundled doctrine is distilled from the build corpus identified in `references/source-provenance.md`. Technical and regulatory claims vary in authority, date, jurisdiction, facility, cultivar, stage, substrate, equipment, and measurement method. Verify consequential claims against current primary sources and local approved procedures before action.

## Source and release context

CanopyOps was authored as a Collaborative Dynamics Augment from the source families and build decisions recorded in the package provenance files and `BUILD-WEEK.md`. Those records explain origin and scope; they do not convert reference material into current operating authority. The installed skill should preserve source class, applicable date, jurisdiction, measurement method, and uncertainty whenever those facts can change a cultivation decision.

## Responsibility boundary

The accountable operator remains responsible for facility policy, worker and crop safety, approved inputs, jurisdictional requirements, laboratory interpretation, equipment operation, and release decisions. Use fictional or sanitized material for demonstrations. Before sharing facility records with an AI host, review `DATA-AND-PRIVACY.md`, `SECURITY.md`, and the selected host's current data controls.
